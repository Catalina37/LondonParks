The Project

The project was developed to inspire and encourage individuals towards an active & healthier lifestyle throughout the year. We present to you (subjectively) the top five Royal Parks of London . So join us for a walk, a stroll or a jog.

The project was created as a result of our training with CodeFirst:Girls, a non profit organisation with the aim to empower and encourage women to join the tech industry.
