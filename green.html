Green Park

For after work sun seekers

Top tips

What to see:

The royal gun salutes, if you happen to be there on the Queen’s or Prince Charles’ Birthday. Also Canada memorial and lots of deck chairs!

Where to refuel:

L’Eto Cafe in Mayfair, it’s a 5 minute walk to get some of the most delicious selection of cakes and teas, the Honey Cake is a must try but be warned, there are queues!

Best Season:

Summer! It’s a hotspot for some after work fizz and crisps among local workers.
