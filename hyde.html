Hyde Park

A great all-rounder

Top tips

What to see:

The Serpentine, Speakers Corner, Winter Wonderland, and whoever’s headlining at the annual music festival BST Hyde Park.

Where to refuel:

You’re in Mayfair and Knightsbridge so it’s going to get fancy. Pick a hotel or head to an renowned institution like Scotts’ for seafood or Zuma for Sushi.

Best Season:

All year round, but ice skating at Winter Wonderland is a magical experience!
