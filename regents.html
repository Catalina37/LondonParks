The Regent's Park

Laid back, arty and elegant

Top tips

What to see:

The Rose Garden, The Open Air Theatre, The view from Primrose Hill, the beautiful Triton Fountain, Avenue Gardens, all the architecture by John Nash and the Tennis Courts.

Where to refuel:

If on the Marylebone side, head to the trendy 108 Brasserie or for Schnitzel at Fischers. If on the Primrose Hill side then Greenberry is great.

Best Season:

Summer, when Frieze Art Fair takes place which makes it busy but great fun.
