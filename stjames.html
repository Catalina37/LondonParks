St James' Park

Quaint and rich in history

Top tips

What to see:

The Mall and Horse Guards Parade, Princess Diana Memorial Walk and of course, Buckingham Palace. The lakes create some serenity at peak times.

Where to refuel:

Ole and Steen in Haymarket for a cosy Scandinavian breakfast experience just 10 minutes away.

Best Season:

Autumn, because the crowds can be unbearable in the summer.
